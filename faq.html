    Frequently Asked Questions:

  • Pricing:

  • Pricing is variable based on the size and type of product. Typically, I have five basic tiers of earrings, which are teeny ($5), small ($8), medium ($12), large ($16), and "animal critters", which are more expensive to make and cost $20. There are other types of products I will be offering, which will be priced individually.

  • Shipping:

  • Shipping is handled per order, usually through USPS First Class for USA orders. Price of shipping will be discussed before you order through either email or DM.

  • Ordering:

  • To make an order, just shoot me a message on either instagram (@sailorstarstuffs, or click the instagram logo on the menu), or my email (on the about page). Describe what pieces you want, or give the number associated with it. Otherwise, if you're local to KC, my stuffs are available at PH Coffee and Transit Coffee.

  • • Returns:

  • Returns are extremely difficult with handmade earrings, as they are tough to sterilize once someone has worn them. I cannot offer returns at this time. However, if you are unhappy with your purchase, please let me know. We will work something out.

  • Ethics:

  • i do my best to make sure all bones & animal remains are ethically sourced. i would never source from animals killed for the sole purpose of commodifying their remains- bones are either sourced secondhand, found personally by me on trips to my local woodlands, or purchased from a local vendor who sources ethically (shoutout to Oracle Natural Science in kc, you can check out their website for more info on sourcing).
